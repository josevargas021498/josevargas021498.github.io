My Long Story Short

One day, after my mother had been in a foreign country for two years persuing a better life for us, I was playing with my
cousin behind his house in my small home town,
Mexico, throwing rocks at bottle targets. It was a great, warm evening...
The sunset was approaching, the clouds were think and spaced out.
I remember a woman looking at me from the hill where my house was set at.
I was blessed to hear the beautiful voice of an angel saying
"Jose, my love!"
It was my mom. My mom and dad never got along once she was back.
My mom, my sister, and I decided to go for a vacation, alone.
Never did I imagine, this vacation would change
my life forever...
I was only 6 years old at the time, and my sister was five,
we traveled to the
Federal District in Mexico, where the big city lights exist.
It was a great adventure since we had never been anywhere
where there were lights on the road, I was used to the dark, deserted dusty road paths back in my town.
We spend a night there and we moved on to the next place. My mom bought us food
from restaurants, "this vacation",
I thought to myself, "it is great! I have never ate at a restaurant before!
Now we eat everyday!".
